SkyShear Privacy Policy

Effective July 17, 2026

SkyShear is a turbulence-forecast viewer. It has no accounts, no sign-in, no advertising, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

What the app uses, and where it goes

  • Location — used on your device to work out which patch of atmosphere you are pointing at. Derived coordinates (latitude/longitude of sky sample points) are sent to the free Open-Meteo weather API to retrieve the forecast. No identifier, account, or device information accompanies those requests, and SkyShear does not store your location anywhere.
  • Motion & orientation sensors — processed entirely on your device to compute where the phone is pointing. Never transmitted.
  • Camera (optional) — if you enable the AR background, the camera image is shown on screen only. It is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

What we store

Nothing about you. The app caches its own program files on your device so it opens quickly (standard web-app caching). That cache contains no personal data and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear site data.

Third parties

The only external service contacted for data is Open-Meteo (their terms); fonts load from Google Fonts. Neither receives any personal information from SkyShear beyond the technical minimum of an HTTP request.
