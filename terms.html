What SkyShear is — and is not

SkyShear is an informational and educational tool that visualizes model-forecast turbulence potential derived from public weather-model data. It is not a certified aviation weather product, is not approved by any aviation authority, and must never be used for flight planning, flight decisions, or any safety-of-life purpose. Pilots must use official aviation weather sources.
